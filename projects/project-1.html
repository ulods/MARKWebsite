College Management System

Our project page

Project Description

This is Web based Project on College Management System, which provided a lot of facility to their user. The objective and scope of College Management System is to record the details various activities of user.

It will simplify the task and reduce the paper work. The system is very user friendly and it is anticipated that functions of the system will be easily accessed by administrators, academics, and applicants.

The application will enable a Administration to have his own account and can easily carry out the Admission procedure. The idea has been designed keeping in mind the ease with which the amount can be credited to a college’s account at the time of admission.
